My Hobbies

Going to the gym

I have been going to the gym for about 6 months now and I guess I can call this a hobbie. I try to go as often as I can when I have free time. Ive come to really enjoy it, great way to decompress, destress and stay healthy.

Collecting Vinyl Records

I have about 50+ vinyl records. Im a big fan of oldies, but I also have vinyl records of newer artists. It's just something about playing vinyl records that makes you feel like you lived in those eras. Whenever I have the time I go to stores that sell vinyls and just browse around and maybe listen to a couple if its available.
